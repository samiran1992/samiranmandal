Samiran Mandal

I am a Post Doctoral Scholar at the Stennis Space Research Center, NASA through The University of Southern Mississippi physical oceanography graduate student at Scripps Institution of Oceanography/UCSD, and the recipient of a Fulbright student fellowship. My thesis work is advised by Nathalie Zilberman and Janet Sprintall and combines observations from a number of observing systems (Argo, HR-XBT, Satellite altimetry) to estimate transport and variability in western boundary currents (WBCs). Originally from New Zealand, I completed my BSc at the University of Otago in 2017 and did a further Honours year at the University of Auckland in 2018.
